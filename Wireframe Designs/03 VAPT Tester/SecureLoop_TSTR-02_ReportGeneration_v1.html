Penetration Test — Executive Summary

Engagement: Q1 Payment Platform VAPT Period: Jan 08 – Jan 22, 2026 Tester: Aliya Khan

Engagement overview

Between Jan 08 and Jan 22 2026, our team conducted a comprehensive penetration test of Acme Corp's payment platform, covering 24 in-scope assets across authentication, API, and data layers.

The engagement identified 3 Critical, 7 High, 12 Medium, and 5 Low severity findings. Three Critical findings require immediate attention as they represent pathways to unauthorised access to customer payment data.

Key risks identified

The most significant risk is an SQL injection vulnerability on the primary authentication endpoint, which can be exploited without authentication to extract customer credentials and payment metadata.

Additionally, excessive IAM permissions on production service accounts create a lateral-movement risk if any individual account is compromised.

Recommended actions

We recommend addressing the three Critical findings within the 7-day SLA window, initiating the high-severity remediation plan within 14 days, and accepting or remediating the remaining medium/low findings within 30 days.
