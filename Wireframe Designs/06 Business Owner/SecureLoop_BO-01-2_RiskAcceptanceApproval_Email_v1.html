A risk in an area you own — Customer Payment Processing — needs a business decision. Priya (Control Owner) has proposed accepting it for 90 days while the replacement component ships.

High Risk Customer Payment Processing Expires 20 Jul 2026
Payment gateway is running on software the vendor no longer patches.
Keeping it live while we migrate means customers can keep paying — but an attacker finding a new flaw in this software would face fewer defences than on a current version.
What this means for the business

The software powering checkout runs a version the vendor stopped supporting last quarter — no new security fixes available. Finance and Ops need the quarterly close cycle to complete before we can swap without freezing card processing. Replacement is in staging, go-live week 37.

Worst-case fallout if exploited before migration: £1.8M – £2.4M plus roughly 18–36 hours of card processing downtime. We've added a WAF rule, restricted outbound egress, and put the gateway under 24/7 watch to reduce the odds.

Compensating controls in place
  • WAF rule tuned to block known exploits
  • Outbound egress restricted from gateway
  • 24/7 SOC watch-rule on gateway traffic
  • Replacement component signed off in staging · go-live week 37
