Justification (578 chars)
This database stores historical transaction backups for audit purposes only. It's scheduled for full replacement in Q3 as part of the infrastructure modernisation program (BUILD-2024-088). Migrating the existing dataset to an encrypted platform before the scheduled replacement would require approximately 6 weeks of engineering effort and $180K in temporary compute costs, which has not been budgeted. The data is already behind a dedicated private subnet with no internet exposure, accessible only to three service accounts via mutual TLS. Access logging is enabled and reviewed weekly.
